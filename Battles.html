Battle of Waterloo – June 18, 1815

Famous Battles

Napoleon commanded armies across Europe. These were some of his most significant battles:

  • Battle of Austerlitz (1805): Often called his greatest victory, defeating Russian and Austrian forces.
  • Battle of Borodino (1812): Fought in Russia, it was one of the bloodiest days of the Napoleonic Wars.
  • Battle of Waterloo (1815): His final defeat, leading to his exile to Saint Helena.
