Rise to Power

Napoleon’s journey began in Corsica. Rising through the military ranks during the French Revolution, he became First Consul of France in 1799 and Emperor in 1804. His ambition and reforms reshaped Europe.

Reforms and Leadership

He introduced the Napoleonic Code, centralized administration, improved education, and emphasized merit. These reforms had lasting impacts beyond his reign, influencing legal systems across the world.
